# 🗂️ Table of Contents: 04-Communication-Skills

This section is dedicated to the skills that govern how we transmit and receive information. Mastering communication is fundamental to building relationships, sharing ideas, and leading effectively.

## Core Topics

1.  **[Public Speaking](./public-speaking/README.md)**
    *   A guide to commanding a room, engaging an audience, and delivering a message with impact.
        *   **[Pitching Ideas to Non-Technical Stakeholders](./public-speaking/pitching-ideas-to-non-tech/README.md)**

2.  **[Storytelling](./storytelling/README.md)**
    *   Learn the art of conveying ideas through narrative, building emotional connection, and making your message memorable.

3.  **[Listening](./listening/README.md)**
    *   A deep dive into active listening, the most underrated communication skill for building trust and understanding.

4.  **[Non-Verbal Communication](./non-verbal-communication/README.md)**
    *   Explore what you're saying without words, from body language to tone of voice, and how to interpret the cues of others.

5.  **[Writing](./writing/README.md)**
    *   A guide to effective writing, focusing on clarity, conciseness, and a structured process for turning thoughts into impactful prose.

6.  **[Visualisation](./visualisation/README.md)**
    *   An exploration of how to use mental and physical images to communicate and prepare.
        *   **[Diagramming for Clarity](./visualisation/diagramming/README.md):** How to use visuals like the C4 model to explain complex systems.
        *   **[Mental Rehearsal for Success](./visualisation/mental-rehearsal/README.md):** The practice of visualizing positive outcomes to program your mind for success.
        *   **[Negative Visualisation for Resilience](./visualisation/negative-visualisation/README.md):** The Stoic practice of imagining negative outcomes to build mental toughness.
