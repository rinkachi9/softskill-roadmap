# 🗂️ Table of Contents: thinking skills

This section focuses on the cognitive skills related to how you process information, reason, solve problems, and learn.
Mastering these skills allows you to navigate complexity with clarity and logic.

## Core Topics

1. **[Critical Thinking](./critical-thinking/README.md)**
    * The art of analyzing information objectively to form a reasoned judgment. Learn the stages of a thinker and the
      barriers that hold you back.

2. **[Problem-Solving](./problem-solving/README.md)**
    * A guide to structured problem-solving, from defining the real problem to implementing and verifying a solution.

3. **[Decision-Making](./decision-making/README.md)**
    * Explore the 7-step process for making effective choices and learn about different mental models like DDDM,
      Decision Trees, and SDT.

4. **[Analysis](./analysis/README.md)**
    * The skill of deconstructing complex information to understand its underlying structure. This section includes:
        * **[Prediction](./analysis/prediction/README.md):** Forecasting future outcomes based on data.
        * **[Pattern Recognition](./analysis/pattern-recognition/README.md):** Identifying meaningful regularities and
          trends.

5. **[Mental Agility](./mental-agility/README.md)**
    * The ability to think flexibly, adapt to change, and pivot your mental approach when a strategy isn't working.

6. **[Learning](./learning/README.md)**
    * A deep dive into the "meta-skill" of learning how to learn. This section covers:
        * **[Active Learning](./learning/active-learning/README.md):** Engaging with material to create durable
          knowledge.
        * **[Spaced Repetition](./learning/spaced-repetition/README.md):** The science of not forgetting.
        * **[Deliberate Practice](./learning/deliberate-practice/README.md):** The path from amateur to expert.
        * **[Meta-Learning](./learning/meta-learning/README.md):** The art of designing your own effective learning
          process.

7. **[Observation](./observation/README.md)**
    * The skill of actively and consciously acquiring information from your environment using all your senses.
