## A Toolbox of Techniques

2.  **[The Pomodoro Technique](./techniques/pomodoro.md)**
    *   A method for maintaining high focus and preventing burnout by working in short, timed intervals.

3.  **[Kanban](./techniques/kanban.md)**
    *   A visual method for managing workflow, limiting work-in-progress, and identifying bottlenecks.

4.  **[Getting Things Done (GTD)](./techniques/gtd.md)**
    *   A complete system for capturing, clarifying, and organizing all of your tasks to achieve a "mind like water."

5.  **[Eat That Frog](./techniques/eat-that-frog.md)**
    *   A simple rule for overcoming procrastination by tackling your most important task first thing in the morning.

6.  **[Timeboxing and Time Blocking](./techniques/timeboxing-and-time-blocking.md)**
    *   Techniques for scheduling your day by allocating specific blocks of time to specific tasks.

7.  **[The Eisenhower Matrix](./techniques/eisenhower-matrix.md)**
    *   A powerful framework for prioritizing tasks based on their urgency and importance.

8.  **[The 4Ds of Productivity](./techniques/4ds.md)**
    *   A quick decision-making filter for processing inputs: Do, Defer, Delegate, or Delete.

9.  **[The 1-3-5 Rule](./techniques/1-3-5-rule.md)**
    *   A simple rule for creating a realistic and balanced daily to-do list.
