# 🗂️ Table of Contents: 01-Self-Skills (Intrapersonal)

This section covers the foundational skills related to understanding and managing your inner world. These are the skills of self-awareness, self-regulation, and personal growth that form the bedrock of a successful and fulfilling life.

## Core Topics

1.  **[Self-Awareness](./self-awareness/README.md)**
    *   The ability to see yourself clearly and objectively, understanding your strengths, weaknesses, values, and how others perceive you.

2.  **[Self-Discipline](./self-discipline/README.md)**
    *   The power to regulate your actions and impulses to align with your long-term goals, even when you don't feel motivated.

3.  **[Self-Motivation](./self-motivation/README.md)**
    *   The internal drive to take initiative, persevere through challenges, and pursue goals without external pressure.

4.  **[Self-Control](./self-control/README.md)**
    *   The ability to manage your immediate impulses, emotions, and desires in the moment.

5.  **[Self-Acceptance](./self-acceptance/README.md)**
    *   The art of embracing your whole self, including your flaws and imperfections, without judgment.

6.  **[Self-Actualization](./self-actualization/README.md)**
    *   The journey of realizing and fulfilling your unique potential, based on Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs.

7.  **[Self-Reflection](./self-reflection/README.md)**
    *   The practice of intentionally looking inward to learn from your experiences and understand your own thoughts and feelings.

8.  **[Growth Mindset](./growth-mindset/README.md)**
    *   The belief that your abilities and intelligence can be developed through effort, learning, and persistence.

9.  **[Resilience](./resilience/README.md)**
    *   The psychological strength to bounce back from adversity, failure, and stress, often emerging stronger.

10. **[Patience](./patience/README.md)**
    *   The ability to remain calm and composed in the face of delay, frustration, or hardship.

11. **[Balance](./balance/README.md)**
    *   The dynamic art of managing competing priorities to live a life that feels whole and sustainable.

12. **[Self-Care](./self-care/README.md)**
    *   The intentional actions taken to care for your physical, mental, and emotional health as a prerequisite for success.

13. **[Sacrifice](./sacrifice/README.md)**
    *   The practice of making conscious, meaningful trade-offs to achieve higher-value, long-term goals.

14. **[Consistency](./consistency/README.md)**
    *   The skill of applying steady, focused effort over time, which is the engine of compounding success.
