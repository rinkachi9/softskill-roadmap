# 🗂️ Table of Contents: 03-Social-Skills (Interpersonal)

This section covers the skills related to how you interact and build relationships with others. Mastering these skills is essential for effective collaboration, influence, and creating strong personal and professional bonds.

## Core Topics

1.  **[Empathy](./empathy/README.md)**
    *   The ability to understand and share the feelings of another. This guide covers the three types of empathy and how to cultivate this foundational social skill.

2.  **[Networking](./networking/README.md)**
    *   The art of building authentic, mutually beneficial relationships, with a focus on giving value and fostering genuine connections.

3.  **[Negotiation](./negotiation/README.md)**
    *   A deep dive into the art of reaching agreement, from core concepts to advanced psychological tools.
        *   **[Key Terminology: BATNA, WATNA, ZOPA](./negotiation/terminology/README.md)**
        *   **[Psychological Tools](./negotiation/psychology/README.md)**
        *   **[Dispute Resolution vs. Deal Making](./negotiation/dispute-vs-deal/README.md)**
        *   **[Related Practices: Arbitration and Mediation](./negotiation/related-practices/README.md)**

4.  **[Debating](./debating/README.md)**
    *   A guide to the art of reasoned argument, focusing on logic, evidence, and respect, and distinguishing it from a simple argument.

5.  **[Conflict Resolution](./conflict-resolution/README.md)**
    *   A framework for navigating disagreements constructively, turning potential friction into an opportunity for growth and understanding.

6.  **[Teamwork](./teamwork/README.md)**
    *   An exploration of the core pillars of effective teamwork, from psychological safety to the stages of group development.
