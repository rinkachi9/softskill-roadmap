# 🗂️ Table of Contents: Japanese Concepts

This collection delves into a series of profound and practical concepts from Japanese culture that offer wisdom on acceptance, imperfection, perseverance, and living in the present moment.

## Core Topics

1.  **[Ichigo Ichie (一期一会)](./ichigo-ichie.md)**
    *   "One time, one meeting." The art of treasuring the unrepeatable nature of every moment.

2.  **[Wabi-Sabi (侘寂)](./wabi-sabi.md)**
    *   The beauty of imperfection, impermanence, and incompletion.

3.  **[Shoshin (初心)](./shoshin.md)**
    *   The "Beginner's Mind." Approaching life with openness, eagerness, and a lack of preconceptions.

4.  **[Oubaitori (桜梅桃李)](./oubaitori.md)**
    *   A reminder to not compare yourself to others, as everyone grows and blooms at their own pace.

5.  **[Shuhari (守破離)](./shuhari.md)**
    *   The three stages of mastery: protect the fundamentals, break the rules, and finally, separate from tradition.

6.  **[Kishi Kaisei (起死回生)](./kishi-kaisei.md)**
    *   "Wake from death and return to life." The spirit of making a dramatic comeback from a hopeless situation.

7.  **[Mono no aware (物の哀れ)](./mono-no-aware.md)**
    *   The gentle, wistful sadness at the impermanence of all things.

## Concepts of Endurance and Acceptance

8.  **[Nana korobi ya oki (七転び八起き)](./nana-korobi-ya-oki.md)**
    *   "Fall down seven times, get up eight." The embodiment of resilience.

9.  **[Ganbaru (頑張る)](./ganbaru.md)**
    *   The spirit of "doing one's best" and persevering through hardship.

10. **[Gaman (我慢)](./gaman.md)**
    *   Enduring the seemingly unbearable with patience and dignity.

11. **[Shikata ga nai (仕方がない)](./shikita-ga-nai.md)**
    *   "It cannot be helped." The wisdom of accepting what is beyond your control.

12. **[Mushin (無心)](./mushin.md)**
    *   "No-mind." The state of effortless action and fluid thought, free from ego and fear.
